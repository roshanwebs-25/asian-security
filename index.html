About Us

Building Safer, Smarter & Better Managed Facilities.

Asian Security & Facility Services Pvt Ltd was founded in Hyderabad in 2018 under the leadership of Mr. Shreekar Reddy. What began as a focused security venture has grown into a full-spectrum integrated facility management company serving over 100 reputed clients across India.

With 3000+ trained professionals, government skill development partnerships and a culture obsessed with discipline and detail, we partner with enterprises, hospitality groups, hospitals, institutions and government organisations to keep their people, premises and operations running flawlessly.
