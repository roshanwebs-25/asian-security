Building Safer, Smarter & Better Managed Facilities

Founded in Hyderabad in 2018, Asian Security & Facility Services has grown into a trusted integrated facility management partner serving 100+ reputed clients with 3000+ trained professionals across India.

Company Overview

Eight years of operational excellence, written shift by shift.

We started in 2018 with a single conviction — that Indian businesses deserve facility management partners who combine global process discipline with on-ground accountability. From a focused security venture, we have grown into a full-spectrum integrated facility management company spanning manned guarding, housekeeping, pest management, gardening, engineering and technical services.

Every contract we deliver is engineered on three pillars: trained people, documented processes and measurable outcomes. That is what allows us to serve marquee brands across hospitality, IT, government, healthcare, education and industry with the same exacting standard.

CEO's Message

"Our work is invisible when it is done right — that is the standard we hold ourselves to."

Mr. Shreekar Reddy — Founder & Chief Executive Officer

From day one, our aspiration has been to build an institution, not just a service provider. That meant investing in training, technology and team culture before we invested in scale. Today, 3000+ colleagues wear our uniform with pride and 100+ clients renew their trust in us year after year. We are deeply grateful — and we are just getting started.

As we expand across India, our promise to every client remains the same: relentless discipline on the ground, transparent communication off it, and an unwavering commitment to your safety, your assets and your people.
